 Library Day

National Librarian's Day is celebrated on August 12th of every month in remembrance of Sri S. R. Ranganathan (12th August 1892 – 27th September 1972), Father of Library Science in India.

The Government of India awarded Padmashri to Dr. S.R. Ranganathan for valuable contributions to Library Science.

Five Laws of Library Science:

  • Books are for use
  • Every reader his/her book
  • Every book, its reader
  • Save the time of the reader
  • A library is a growing organism
